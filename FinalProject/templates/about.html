About eZay's Creators

Mitchell Hewitt

During the early stages of eZay's creation, Mitchell walked into the woods and wasn't heard from for a week. After 7 days and 7 nights of sitting next to a bubbling stream (and ordering a new a bike from eBay on his mobile phone), the genius idea that is now eZay came to him.

Keith Saunders

Keith grew up in a mountain community of web programmer monks. His entire youth was spent training with 1 goal in mind, to create the best online shopping experience possible. He joined the eZay team after meeting Mitchell at CS conference.

Ahmad Meer

Master hacker Ahmad was a notorious troll that would hack large companies sites just for fun (and a little profit). He was then contacted by an anonymous person online who hired him to hack into eZay. After easily completing the job, his online client revealed himself to be Peter Bellagh, head of eZay security. Ahmad was then hired on the spot to beef up eZay's online security.

Peter Bellagh

In his early years, Peter drifted from town to town as a sell-sword. One day he ran into a warrior-programmer monk named Keith and challenged him to a battle. After the battle ended in a draw, Keith offered him the job of guarding the eZaY super vault. Peter has not left his post since.
